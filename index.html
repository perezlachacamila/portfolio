I am a conductor and choral leader with a passion for promoting music by female composers. I was part of the Argentine panel at the III International Symposium of Female Conductors (2020) and, in 2021, received the National Arts Fund Creation Grant to record an album with my choir, Anthorium Vocal. This project won the Gold Award in the "Mixed Choirs" category at the AAMCANT Latin American Choral Music Competition. In 2022, I was awarded the Mozarteum Argentino Mecenazgo Scholarship to study choral symphonic repertoire and opera with Maestro Carlos Vieu.
